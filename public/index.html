Proctoring Rules

Please review the proctoring guidelines before starting the session.

  • Full Screen Lock

    Enforces fullscreen. Exiting triggers an immediate warning and pauses the session.

  • Copy-Paste Block

    Right-click context menus, copy/paste, and Developer Tools keyboard shortcuts are deactivated.

  • Tab & Focus Tracking

    Navigating to other windows, switching browser tabs, or losing focus triggers warning popups.

  • Webcam & Eyeball Gaze Tracking

    AI face count tracking + MediaPipe 478-point eyeball iris mesh analysis flags sideways looking away or head turn actions.

  • Compulsory Secondary Feed

    Connecting a secondary webcam or phone camera stream is mandatory. Scanned every 3s via Groq Vision API for mobile device usage.

  • Secondary Monitors

    Connecting secondary screens or extended displays is prohibited and checked continuously.

  • Hardware Source Checks

    Muting or disabling your webcam/microphone at the hardware source triggers warnings.

  • Audio Noise Analysis

    Continuous background speech, whispering, or prompter voices trigger audio warnings.

  • Strict Violation Limits

    General violations allow 5 strikes max, while secondary feed mobile phone detection terminates on the 3rd strike.

Attempts to bypass security features are recorded in the final evaluation report.
